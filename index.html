Welcome to TitleTown Events LCC!

Welcome

TitleTown Events LCC is your gateway to the ultimate collegiate sporting events and training experiences in the stunning backdrop of Hawaii! We specialize in curating unforgettable journeys that bring together the excitement of collegiate sports and the tropical allure of Hawai'i. Whether you're a college team aiming to participate in a sporting event or looking to enhance your training regimen in a paradisiacal setting, we are your dedicated partner in turning these aspirations into reality.

Who We Are

TitleTown Events LLC is excited to be in contact withyou and help reach your team goals in playing inHawai’i.

As of March 2023, TitleTown Events LLC took over for Malika Sports Tours at Legendary Travel. Malika Sports Tours had a successful 23-year career in Hawai’i collegiate sporting events and we look forward to continuing that with you and your University. In those 23 years, Malika Sports Tours has sent teams from 44 of the 50 states to Hawai’i. Those teams have played in O’ahu and on the Big Island.

Reminder: TitleTown Events LLC Basketball Tournaments from October through February are official Qualifiers for the 2024 Middle School Basketball Championship to be held on campus at Mizzou and other top facilities in Columbia in March of 2024. The Top 2 teams in each division will qualify for the State Championship to battle top teams from towns throughout Missouri.

What We Do

Run and coordinate collegiate sporting events in Hawaii for smaller athletic divisions: D2, D3, NAIA, NCCAA Basketball, Softball, Soccer We streamline the travel services for the team: Lodging, Ground Transportation, Activities (All condensed to travel packages) Work with individual companies to aid in other parts of travel: Airlines (Kupar Tours) External Fan/Parent Travel (Maryville Trave)
