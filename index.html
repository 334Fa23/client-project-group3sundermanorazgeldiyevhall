    • Welcome

    TitleTown Events LLC is excited to be in contact withyou and help reach your team goals in playing inHawai’i. As of March 2023, TitleTown Events LLC took over forMalika Sports Tours at Legendary Travel. Malika SportsTours had a successful 23-year career in Hawai’icollegiate sporting events and we look forward tocontinuing that with you and your University. In those 23 years, Malika Sports Tours has sent teamsfrom 44 of the 50 states to Hawai’i. Those teams haveplayed in O’ahu and on the Big Island.

    TIMELINE

    1

    Event Set up

    Once we confirm with the Hawai’i Universities and tournament directors about events we start to create flyers and advertisement to get colleges interested in making these events happen!

    2

    Initial Contact

    This is where we are at now! In this stage we will begin our relationship as we get to know one another and learn more about your teams needs while on the Island.

    3

    Travel Package

    Once you have reviewed the information from the intial contact and have decided to particpate in this event, it is time to choose one of our three travel packages.

    4

    Contract Signed

    Once we confirm with you on the travel package we will then get a contract sent up. An initial deposit is due 30 days after signed contract. Then mark your calendars and look forward to experiencing the great state of Hawai’i.

    5

    Land in Hawai'i

    Transportation will pick you up at the airport and will take you to the hotel. From there your experience is just beginning. Your games, practices, and activities will be awaiting you in the upcoming days.

    6

    Travel Home

    Unfortunately, your time in paradise is over. We look forward to seeing you comeback in the future!
