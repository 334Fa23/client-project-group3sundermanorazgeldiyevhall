Ground Transportation

ARRIVE SAFELY TO HOTEL

All ground transportation is a round trip affair. Ground transportation on the Big Island is done by our staff and an outside company. On O'ahu, ground transportation is done through Hawaii Super Transit. Wilbert and his staff are top notch! He provides your team with a comfortable ride in a well air-conditioned motor coach that will pick you up at the airport and take you to your hotel and activities while you are still on the Island.

Lodging

COMFORT WHILE YOU STAY

Lodging in Hilo is done right in the heart of Hilo Bay! The Hilo Hawaiian Hotel is a beautiful estate that sits right on the Hilo Bay near the Lil'uokalani Gardens and Coconut Island. The Hilo Hawaiian Hotel also houses the only steakhouse in Hilo, WSW Restaurant, and also has the Wai'oli Lounge which includes appetizers and live music. Lodging on O'ahu is done right in the middle of Waikiki at the Courtyard by Marriott Waikiki Beach. Brent and his staff at the Courtyard provide an excellent stay and are close to everything! Just a five minute walk to Waikiki Beach.

Activities

FUN WHILE YOU STAY

Along with your games and practices, your team will get the opportunity to see the historic sites of O'ahu. TitleTown offers three different travel packages; Basic, Standard, and Premium. Those travel packages can be found on the next page! If you want to stay in Hilo for the entire stay we can create activity packages for you to fit your team activity needs on the Big Island. Other accommodations can also be arranged before the contract is signed!

Explore Hawai'i

Get the whole Hawai’i experience while you’re here with a hike up to Diamond Head or Makapu’u Lighthouse in Oahu, take a tour to the USS Arizona Memorial in Pearl Harbor, or attend the Paradise Cove Luau. Some or all of these activities will be included in your travel package.
